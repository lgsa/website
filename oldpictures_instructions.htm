Section 2
You have a digital picture or you are able to scan your picture
 
You need to attach it to an e-mail to send us. Don't try to re-size or crop it. Just send it as it is. Click here to start creating the e-mail. Write a brief description including the year or even the full date if known. Attach your picture to the e-mail and send it off.

We will acknowledge that it has arrived.

 


Section 3

Write on a piece of paper your name and address. Add the year or full date of the picture and a description about it, and post it off in a padded envelope to:
